# Meine Deutschlernreise 🇩🇪

> A structured, self-paced German A1 study plan — from absolute zero to Goethe A1 certification in 24 weeks.

Inspired by [coding-interview-university](https://github.com/jwasham/coding-interview-university). This is my multi-month study plan for achieving CEFR A1 in German using free resources + Netzwerk Neu A1.

---

## What is it?

This is a **checkbox-tracked study plan** that takes you from knowing zero German to passing the Goethe-Zertifikat A1 exam. It combines:

- **Netzwerk Neu A1** (textbook backbone — 12 chapters over 24 weeks)
- **DW Nico's Weg** (free video course that parallels the textbook)
- **Coffee Break German** (podcast for daily listening)
- **Anki** (spaced repetition for vocabulary)
- **Fun resources** (YouTube, TV shows, music, games)

**Timeline:** 24 weeks (4-6 months) at 1-2 hours/day.

---

## How to use it

1. Fork this repo
2. Mark items with `[x]` as you complete them
3. Follow the weekly blocks in order, top to bottom
4. Never skip Anki — even on rest days
5. Use the daily routine templates below to structure your time

## Study Streams Explained

Your daily study is split into **5 parallel streams**. Each serves a purpose:

| Stream | Purpose | Primary Resource | Daily Time |
|--------|---------|-----------------|------------|
| **Core Textbook** | Structure, grammar progression | Netzwerk Neu A1 | 20-30 min |
| **Listening** | Comprehensible input, ear training | Nico's Weg + Coffee Break German | 15-20 min |
| **Vocabulary** | Build word bank via spaced repetition | Anki | 15-20 min |
| **Grammar Drill** | Practice new patterns | Schubert-Verlag, Lingolia | 10-15 min |
| **Fun/Immersion** | Motivation, cultural exposure | YouTube, shows, music, games | 10-15 min |

## Key Principles

1. **Consistency > Intensity** — 30 minutes every day beats 4 hours on Saturday
2. **Never skip Anki** — Even 5 minutes on a bad day. This is the one non-negotiable habit.
3. **Comprehensible input** — Listen to things just slightly above your level
4. **Active recall > passive review** — Test yourself, don't just re-read notes
5. **Speak from Day 1** — Read aloud, shadow podcasts, talk to yourself
6. **Fun is mandatory** — Every week includes something enjoyable. Burnout kills progress.
7. **YAGNI for perfection** — You don't need perfect grammar to communicate. Progress > perfection.
8. **Track your progress** — Check those boxes. Visual progress is motivating.
